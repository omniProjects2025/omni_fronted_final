Our Empanelments

We partner with a wide range of leading insurance providers, third-party administrators (TPAs), corporates and government health schemes to ensure patients receive the medical care they need without financial stress. These empanelments enable eligible patients to access cashless treatments and hassle-free claims, making their healthcare journey smoother and more affordable. Our dedicated insurance helpdesk is available at all branches to assist with documentation, approvals and clarifications. So you can focus on your recovery, not the paperwork.

What is empanelment in a hospital?

Empanelment refers to the hospital's tie-up with insurance companies, TPAs, corporates, or government health schemes to offer cashless or reimbursable medical services to eligible patients.

How do I know if my insurance is accepted at your hospital?

You can check the list of empanelled partners on this page or call our insurance helpdesk. Our team will verify your insurance provider and guide you through the eligibility process.
